Powers

Record the novice power of your first origin, the novice power of your second origin , and if you are 3rd level or higher, the utility power of your choice.

Ability Score and Modifier

You get an 18 in the primary ability score of your first origin and a 16 for your second origin (20 if they match).

Roll 3d6 for each remaining score. Next, see the ability modifier chart for each score and enter that value + your level.

Skills

Each origin provides a skill bonus, and you gain a +4 bonus to one random skill; see the skill bonus table. To make a skill check, roll a d20 and add your level, your ability score modifie for the skills key ability, any bonuses you have from the origin, and any bonuses or penalties from your gear, conditions, or from the situation.

Gear

Choose your armour and weapon. At least 1st level, you have one explorer's kit (and everything in it). You also make 1d4 + 1 rolls on the starting gear table. You can also trade in your explorer's kit for an extra roll, use the Get Gear button twice.

Hit Points

Determine your hit points and speed.

Speed is 6 plus any modifiers for your origin.

Defense

Determine your defenses. Your defenses are equal to 10 + your level + the better of two scores. Also for AC, add armour bonus + shield bonus, if any, and ony add Dex or Int if wearing light or no armour.

Attacks

Your attack bonus is your ability modiefier + your level + accuracy bonus for the wapon or power. Your damage bonus uses the same ability modifier as the attack bonus + either your level or twice your level. Also fill in your initive score.
